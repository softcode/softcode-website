About us

We develop and manage tailor-made, secure software solutions that creates value in critical business processes and customer products.

What

Our primary focus is industries and organizations
with specific demands in quality, security and
availability. This typically includes Manufacturing,
Public transportation, Energy, Logistics, Telecom
and Public sector.

How

With a combination of expertise in Software
engineering and an extensive industry
understanding, we deliver high-quality project
services, application life-cycle management, and
top-tier resources.

Who

Our teams are located in the Nordics. We believe that customer proximity, both in relations and geographically, creates the best outcomes and business value for our customers.

Although we are a tech company, everything starts with the people. We are a diverse team of experts who love our workplace and are passionate to create value for our customers. As a team and individually, we always live by our core values;

  • Curiosity
  • Diligence
  • Reliability

Softcode is a privately held company that was founded 2012, headquartered in Karlstad, Sweden.

Swedish Railway

Sweden's first and market-leading railway operator. Connecting Sweden, Norway and the capitals of Scandinavia.

  • Sweden

How we made the difference

Softcode was engaged in 2015 to refactor a critical business operations application. Since then, the partnership has gradually expanded to include the development of several new software solutions, the modernization of the railway operations software suite, lifecycle management responsibilities, 24/7 support agreements, and more.

Over the years, Softcode has developed or collaborated on a multitude of different software solutions for SJ. These assignments span multiple business units at SJ, including railway operations, train maintenance, traffic planning, regulatory compliance, and others.

Karlstad University

More than 17 500 students and scientists. Conducts research and provide education of importance to society.

  • 2024-ongoing
  • Sweden
  • Research and Development

How we made the difference

Softcode has been appointed to a research project to evaluate immersive education solutions over B5G/6G networks. The project will use the IMAGINE-B5G testbed at the University of Oslo (Norway) to explore how AR/VR technologies can enhance teacher-student remote communication.

The project is carried out by Karlstad University and Softcode in a partnership. Anna Brunstrom, professor in Computer Science at Karlstad University, says that the project will “deliver practical insights on various educational scenarios, including engineering, art, and healthcare training.

Critical Communications Company

Multinational supplier of critical communication and platforms for emergency authorities, military and off-shore operations.

  • 2024-ongoing
  • Sweden
  • Software Assessment & Strategy Development

How we made the difference

Softcode was engaged to analyze and recommend improvements to a critical communication platform used by emergency (“blue light”) authorities across several Nordic countries. The Software Assessment identified structural issues and highlighted the need for significant quality and security improvements.

The assessment also underscored the customer's need for an established Software Development Strategy to support the systems and software-based services they deliver. Softcode was subsequently engaged to lead the strategy development project as subject matter experts in this domain. The strategy will be tailored to the customer’s business needs, risk tolerance, and regulatory demands.

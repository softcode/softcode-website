Services

We work with tailor-made and business critical software solutions, where Quality and Security is vital.

1 Resources

We provide top-tier resources with extensive experience in business logic, architecture, most programming languages, testing, Machine Learning and AI. Our resources can be engaged individually or as teams, for long- or short term assignments.

2 Project Services

We deliver software strategy development, software assessments, refactorization, development, testing and other packaged and/or tailor-made project services. Our project services are delivered by senior experts and well-proven methodology.

We offer services covering the entire lifecycle, from exploration and defining requirements to decommissioning. This includes long-term commitments for continuous maintenance, improvements and support, delivered in line with industry standards and established frameworks.
